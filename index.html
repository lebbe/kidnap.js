kidnap.js

for all your online shady GUI business

kidnap.js is a JavaScript plugin for converting your dull ordinary text into a badass letter of blackmail. Each letter looks like it is cut out from a magazine or newspaper, and glued on a piece of paper.

Example

We have kidnapped your dog. Pay up $100 or we will feed him cat-food.
